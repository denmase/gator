📚 Example Queries

Click on any example to load and execute it:

1. Rata-rata Outstanding Balance per Departemen

Group by department, hitung rata-rata outstanding balance dari semua kredit.

Group By: department | Aggregations: avg(credits.outstanding_balance)

2. Total Initial Limit Kredit per Kota (Credit Card Only)

Filter untuk product_type = "credit card", group by city, hitung total initial limit dan jumlah kredit.

Where: credits.product_type = "credit card" | Group By: city | Aggregations: sum(credits.initial_limit), count(*)

3. Maximum Delinquency Overall

Tanpa group by, hitung maximum delinquency dari semua kredit.

Aggregations: max(credits.delinquency)

4. Worst Delinquency in Last 6 Months per Department

Menganalisis historis delinquency 6 bulan terakhir.

Group By: department | Special: worst_delinquency (last 6 months from credits.last_24_delq_hist)

5. Max Collectability Code in Last 3 Months

Melihat collectability terburuk dalam 3 bulan terakhir.

Special: max_collectability (last 3 months from credits.last_24_coll_hist)

6. Jumlah Akun Dibuka dalam 3 Bulan Terakhir

Filter kredit yang dibuka dalam 3 bulan terakhir.

Where: credits.open_date > (3 months ago) | Aggregations: count(*)

7. Kredit dengan Collectability Code Buruk (3, 4, atau 5)

Filter untuk mendeteksi kredit bermasalah.

Where: credits.collectability_code IN [3,4,5] | Aggregations: count(*), sum(credits.outstanding_balance)

8. Statistik Kredit per Product Type

Analisis lengkap per jenis produk kredit.

Group By: credits.product_type | Aggregations: count(*), avg(credits.outstanding_balance), max(credits.delinquency)
